Here's what you need to know:

1. Quiz Structure: The quiz consists of 5 multiple-choice questions. Each question has four possible answers, but only one is correct.

2. Scoring: Each correct answer earns you points. There are no penalties for incorrect answers, so feel free to guess if you're unsure!

3. Results: At the end of the quiz, you'll receive your score.

How to Play:

1. Click the "Take QUIZ" section from menu. On the code input enter one of two available codes for the moment: SCQ1 or SCQ2. Complete with your name too on the other input.

2. Read each question carefully and select the answer you believe is correct.

3. Once you've answered all the questions, click "Submit" to see your results.

Enjoy the quiz and may the best brain win!
